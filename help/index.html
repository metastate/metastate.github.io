Subscription

Is account registration required?

Account registration for Platonic is required to login and maintain purchases history. This ensures a valid communication channel for all parties involved in any transactions.

I want to manage my subscriptions!

Follow this link to manage your subscriptions
Link: Apple Support

Turn off auto-renewal or cancel my subscription?

To turn off auto-renewable subscription, please follow the support link associated with the subscription on your account.
Link: Apple Support

How to check the subscription status?

You can check the status of your trial or subscription through the iTunes account settings on your device.
Please see these instructions for canceling through your mobile device Settings:

1. On your iPhone or iPad, open Settings
2. Select iTunes & App Store
3. Tap your Apple ID at the top of the screen and then tap View Apple ID. Sign in if requested to do so
4. This brings you to an Account Settings page, where you can select the Subscriptions option and view your Platonic subscription.
5. Once there, you should see the Platonic subscription listed.
6. If the subscription has been canceled, you will see the expiration date listed. If it has not been cancelled yet, you'll see the renewal date, along with the price you'll be charged at and a "Cancel Subscription" button.

I want a refund what can I do?

You will need to contact Apple iTunes Store in order to get a refund.
Please contact Apple iTunes Customer Support directly at Apple Support.
Account

How do I delete my account?

If you would like to delete your Platonic account altogether, please send us your request to contact@brainstate.app.

How can I change the password on my account?

The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • If your account is already logged in, please logout.To do so, in the app go to, Settings > Account > Logout.
  • Once you press back button from the settings, a login screen will be presented.
  • Choose forgot password to change your password.

Can I update the email address on my account?

Updating a new email address for your account is unfortunately unavailabel at this time. The account is directly tied to your email address for communication and to save purchases history.
Troubleshooting

Can I use Platonic app offline?

Platonic streams audio from cloud storage and requires to be online. unfortunately offline operation is limited to the seven free chapters of the app.
To save space in your device Platonic is designed to stream audio directly and also does not save audio offline other than the free chapters.

Can I use Platonic on more than one device?

You can access Platonic across multiple Apple mobile and tablet devices associated with the same Apple Id.

Audio stopped abruptly while playing, what should I do about it?

Problems regarding stuttered audio, playback delays and playback stopped are caused due to interuption in an internet connection. Please restart the app while making sure your connectivity is strong.
